Who Are We

DEFENBER are a professional and trustworthy cyber security provider based in Singapore which you can rely on keep your business or organisation safe against any form of cyber crime, such as malware infection, cyber espionage, cyber-attacks, data leakage etc.

We believe in providing the most comprehensive, personalized service that caters to each of our clients. We provide high quality and effective service to our clients by understanding their IT infrastructure, needs and challenges.

We have a lot of experienced, highly skilled, and certified cyber security professionals and penetration testers that provide unparalleled cyber security services.

Company History

DEFENBER were founded in 2010 in Singapore by a group of college friends that just graduated who had intrest in starting a cyber security company. By 2017, DEFENBER had become a prominent cyber security company that was ranked 5th best cyber security service provider in Singapore. By 2019, DEFENBER were ranked the 3rd best cyber security service provider in Singapore.
